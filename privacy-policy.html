Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  CA Prabhpreet Singh

We take your privacy seriously — not because we have to, but because it is the right way to work with clients. This page explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We have written it in plain language so you actually know what you are agreeing to.

What Information We Collect

When you contact us, fill out a form, or book a consultation on this website, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your phone number, if you choose to provide it
  • A brief description of your query or tax situation
  • Basic usage data — such as which pages you visited and what browser you use — collected anonymously via analytics

We only collect what is necessary. We do not ask for information we do not need.

How We Use Your Information

The information you share with us is used to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or consultation request
  • Understand your situation well enough to give you relevant guidance
  • Send follow-up communication related to your query
  • Improve the way this website works

We do not send unsolicited marketing emails. If we send a follow-up, it will be relevant to your situation or a tax development that may directly affect you.

Cookies

This website uses cookies — small files stored in your browser — to understand how people use the site and to keep things working smoothly.

When you first visit, you will see a short notice asking for your preference. You can accept all cookies, reject non-essential ones, or manage your choices. You can revisit this at any time using the Cookie Settings link in the footer.

Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. That is a firm commitment, not a formality.

In the course of running this website, we may share limited information with:

  • A scheduling or CRM tool we use to manage client consultations
  • Analytics providers (such as Google Analytics) in anonymised, aggregated form

Any tools we use are bound by their own privacy policies, and we only share what is strictly necessary for them to work.

How We Protect Your Data

We take reasonable and proportionate steps to keep your information secure — including HTTPS encryption, secure hosting, and access controls on internal systems.

No system is completely without risk, but we treat your data with the same care we would want applied to our own.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we update or correct it
  • Ask us to delete it, where applicable under applicable law
  • Withdraw your consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, simply reach out using the contact details below. We will respond promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will change the date at the top of this page. We will not make changes that significantly affect how we handle your data without letting you know.

Get in Touch

If you have any questions about this privacy policy — or about how your information is handled — please feel free to contact us. There are no gatekeepers here.
